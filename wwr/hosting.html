Hosting

"Choose the hosting of your dreams"

At Dry Oar, design your own adventure, not only in the choice of activities but in what type of accommodation you want to experience it.

Hotel

If you prefer the comfort of a room with beautiful views of nature but without losing the warmth of home, this is your option. Personalized attention, private bathroom and optional maid service. Prepared for older people, people with difficulties, children or special attention. Our staff will keep you comfortable and with all needs covered.

Wood Cabins

The cabins are ideal for those who want close contact with nature without losing basic comforts and feeling safe. Ideal for those people who need a comfortable rest or have some limitations. These cabins are supplied with beds, bathroom and running water.

Camping

If you like contact with nature is its maximum expression, this option is for you. Areas prepared for camping, with separate lots so you can choose the best place for you. Although it is rustic and ideal for feeling isolated, the place has security and the possibility of connecting to electricity sources. Bathrooms distributed for the comfort of campers.
